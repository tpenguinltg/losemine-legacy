Losemine

Overview

The object of Minesweeper is to uncover all the squares that do not hold mines as quickly as possible. If you uncover a mine, you lose the game.

Gameplay

  1. On the Game menu, click New.
  2. Click on any square on the playing field to start the timer.

Notes

  • You can uncover a square by clicking it. If you uncover a mine, you lose the game.
  • The number that appears on the squares indicate the number of mines in the eight squares surrounding it.
  • To mark a square that you suspect contains a mine, right-click it.

Customizing the playing field

  1. On the Game menu, click Custom.
  2. Customize the field size by specifying the number of squares horizontally and vertically, and by specifying the number of mines to place on the field.

Strategies and Tips

  • If you are unsure about a square, you can mark it with a question mark (?). Later, you can either remove the markings or mark the square as a mine by right-clicking the square once or twice respectively.
  • When you have flagged all the mines around a numbered square, you can uncover the rest of the squares around it by middle-clicking it or by clicking it with the left and right buttons simultaneously. It will have no effect if too little or too many surrounding squares are flagged.
  • Look for common patterns in numbers as they often indicate a corresponding pattern of mines. For example, the pattern 2-3-2 at the edge of a group of uncovered squares indicates a row of three mines next to the three numbers.
